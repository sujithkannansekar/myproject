This property

Head to the Arctic Hideaway and experience our remote island tiny-house village. This is an architectural collection of 11
individual buildings, with 5 being dedicated to sleeping. The iconic tower house is a shared space along with the kitchen house,
studio building, bathhouse, crab trap and sauna. This is the perfect place to experience the northern lights and midnight sun.
All our food must be shipped from the mainland, so there is an extra fee to cover all meals and access to the pantry.
